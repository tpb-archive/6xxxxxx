This is a cleaned up version of the KM99 archive of WWII radio audio files. I've also added some additional audio files to the archive. :)

This collection of files encompasses the length and breadth of WWII from the consolidation of power by Hitler and Mussolini in the early 1930's to the Nuremberg verdicts of 1946.

It's a fascinating look at history and the history of broadcasting.

- J.
