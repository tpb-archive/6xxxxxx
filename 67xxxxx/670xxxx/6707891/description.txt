Mancini Fires are winning new fans and turning heads with their unique style of Indie/Pop. With a variety of different influences, and songs written from personal experience and based on real life situations, the band offer hope and honesty through their music whilst describing heartbreak and emotions that everyone can relate to. The band display excellent musical ability and versatility when playing live, with the emotion and sound captured on their recordings brought to the live arena.
